

            Qandy Pocket Computer
         Overview of Keyboard Driver



The Qandy emulates a keyboard on the screen and also
intercepts physical keystrokes and processes them making
it it's own computer with it's own input/output functions. 

By pressing SHIFT or CAPS to switch to the upper case
character/symbol set. You can also press the ALT key
and/or SHIFT key to switch to an extended ASCII graphic
character set. 

Combined with the ANSI enabled display rendering the
Qandy is an ideal device for generating and displaying
ANSI art.
