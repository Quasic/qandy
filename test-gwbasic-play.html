GWBASIC PLAY Command Format Test

Testing the new playNotes() function that supports GWBASIC.COM "PLAY [string]" command format.

Basic Tests

Basic melody: playNotes("C D E F G A B")
With octave and length: playNotes("O3 L8 CDEFGAB")
Complex with tempo: playNotes("T100 C#4 D8 F+16")

Sharps and Flats

Sharp notes (#): playNotes("C# D# F# G# A#")
Sharp notes (+): playNotes("C+ D+ F+ G+ A+")
Flat notes (-): playNotes("D- E- G- A- B-")

Tempo Tests

Slow tempo: playNotes("T60 C D E F G")
Fast tempo: playNotes("T200 C D E F G")

Note Lengths

Whole notes: playNotes("L1 C D E")
Half notes: playNotes("L2 C D E F")
Quarter notes: playNotes("L4 C D E F G")
Eighth notes: playNotes("L8 C D E F G A B C")
Mixed lengths: playNotes("C1 D2 E4 F8 G16")

Octaves

Low octave: playNotes("O2 C D E F G")
Middle octave: playNotes("O4 C D E F G")
High octave: playNotes("O6 C D E F G")

Pauses/Rests

With rests: playNotes("C P4 E P4 G")

Background Playback & Looping

Background loop: loopNotes("O4 L8 C E G E")

Musical Examples

C Major Scale: playNotes("L8 C D E F G A B C")
Simple song: playNotes("T120 L4 C C G G A A G2")
